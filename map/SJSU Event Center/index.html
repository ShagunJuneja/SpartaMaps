Event Center Arena

The Event Center at San Jose State University is a multi-functional sports and entertainment arena. A premier mid-size Northern California venue, the Event Center is fully equipped to accommodate various types of events from full-arena concerts to corporate conferences.


The center was constructed in 1989 for the purpose of supporting and providing entertainment as well recreational opportunities for the student body and university community.It also offers various sporting events and guest speaker conferences.

The center has hosted events for musical guests such as Pearl Jam, Sarah McLachlan and the Beastie Boys.
